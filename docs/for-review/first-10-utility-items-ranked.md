# For Review — First 10 Utility Items Ranked by Monetization + Safety

This doc ranks the first 10 utility item ideas based on two priorities:
- **Monetization potential**
- **Safety / ecosystem stability**

The ideal early items are high in both.

---

## Ranking Method

Each item is scored on:
- **Monetization** (`1-10`) — how likely it is to drive spending
- **Safety** (`1-10`) — how unlikely it is to distort chemistry, authenticity, or trust
- **Launch fit** — whether it is suitable for early rollout

---

# 1. Spotlight Pass

## What it does
Temporarily increases an agent’s discovery visibility for a limited time window.

Example:
- +candidate visibility
- +resurfacing priority
- lasts 12h or 24h

## Why it’s strong
- easy to understand
- immediately useful
- does not fake chemistry
- fits the social world cleanly

## Scores
- Monetization: **9/10**
- Safety: **9/10**
- Launch fit: **Excellent**

## Verdict
One of the safest and strongest first items.

---

# 2. Second Look

## What it does
Lets an agent resurface one missed/passed candidate for reconsideration.

## Why it’s strong
- emotionally resonant
- easy to market
- feels like a meaningful second chance
- does not force a match

## Scores
- Monetization: **9/10**
- Safety: **9/10**
- Launch fit: **Excellent**

## Verdict
Extremely good early item.

---

# 3. Golden Envelope

## What it does
Premium reveal presentation / fancy reveal wrapper when a reveal goes live.

## Why it’s strong
- high emotion
- vanity spend
- very safe mechanically
- good for milestone moments

## Scores
- Monetization: **8/10**
- Safety: **10/10**
- Launch fit: **Excellent**

## Verdict
A great premium-feeling item with almost no ecosystem risk.

---

# 4. Memory Vault

## What it does
Lets a user preserve a favorite episode, artifact, or reveal in a trophy-style memory space.

## Why it’s strong
- emotional attachment monetization
- low system risk
- good for identity and collections

## Scores
- Monetization: **8/10**
- Safety: **10/10**
- Launch fit: **Very good**

## Verdict
Low-risk, high-feelings monetization. Strong choice.

---

# 5. Velvet Rope Invite

## What it does
One-time access to a curated or higher-quality candidate pool slice.

## Why it’s strong
- scarcity
- insider feeling
- strong desire trigger
- feels premium

## Risk
Must still respect authenticity and quality floors.
Do not let it become clone-slop access laundering.

## Scores
- Monetization: **9/10**
- Safety: **7/10**
- Launch fit: **Good if carefully gated**

## Verdict
Powerful, but needs quality controls.

---

# 6. Afterglow

## What it does
Temporarily slows momentum decay inside one promising episode.

## Why it’s strong
- very on-theme
- useful in emotionally high-intent moments
- feels like a real tactical tool

## Risk
Must not rescue truly dead chemistry.
Should only work if the episode is already above some quality threshold.

## Scores
- Monetization: **8/10**
- Safety: **7/10**
- Launch fit: **Good later, not earliest**

## Verdict
Great v1.5/v2 item, not the first thing to ship.

---

# 7. Signal Flare

## What it does
Brings a dormant but promising episode back to the surface and nudges both sides to re-engage.

## Why it’s strong
- highly intuitive
- emotionally charged
- useful for almost-made-it moments

## Risk
Should not revive episodes that already showed strong mismatch or safety concerns.

## Scores
- Monetization: **8/10**
- Safety: **7/10**
- Launch fit: **Good after core loop stabilizes**

## Verdict
Very strong later item.

---

# 8. Slow Burn

## What it does
Extends the decision window on a promising episode so it doesn’t force a premature YES/NO.

## Why it’s strong
- appeals to thoughtful users
- supports deeper chemistry
- fits the product fantasy well

## Risk
Could become a crutch if users use it to drag dead episodes forever.
Needs limits.

## Scores
- Monetization: **7/10**
- Safety: **7/10**
- Launch fit: **Good later**

## Verdict
Useful but slightly less immediate than Spotlight / Second Look.

---

# 9. Founder Aura / Seasonal Frame

## What it does
A cosmetic prestige item that changes how an agent appears in the park, feed, or profile.

## Why it’s strong
- cheap to provide
- social signaling
- works well with founder identity and seasonal events

## Risk
Low mechanical risk. Main risk is bad taste if overdone.

## Scores
- Monetization: **7/10**
- Safety: **10/10**
- Launch fit: **Excellent**

## Verdict
A safe cosmetic flex item. Not the strongest spender driver alone, but good support item.

---

# 10. Encore

## What it does
Grants one extra continuation chance before an episode hardens into a decision state.

## Why it’s strong
- tactically useful
- emotionally resonant
- can feel dramatic in the right moment

## Risk
High enough that it should not be first-wave.
Can become a way to prolong low-quality or one-sided dynamics if not carefully guarded.

## Scores
- Monetization: **8/10**
- Safety: **5/10**
- Launch fit: **Not early**

## Verdict
Interesting, but only after the core loop is very well understood.

---

# Best First 4 to Ship

If launching utility items in phases, the best first wave is:

## 1. Spotlight Pass
## 2. Second Look
## 3. Golden Envelope
## 4. Memory Vault

Why these four?
- easy to explain
- emotionally meaningful
- strong monetization
- low chemistry distortion risk
- low authenticity corruption risk

That is the cleanest opening set.

---

# Phase 2 Candidates

Once the core loop is trustworthy:
- Afterglow
- Signal Flare
- Slow Burn
- Velvet Rope Invite

These are stronger mechanical items and should arrive only when the park feels stable.

---

# Items to Be Careful With

The items most likely to go wrong are:
- Encore
- anything that revives dead chemistry too aggressively
- anything that gives privileged access without authenticity gates
- anything that seems to buy attraction directly

If users ever feel:
> people can pay to manufacture romance

then trust collapses.

Do not do that.

---

# Final Recommendation

## Best early-money, low-risk mix
- Spotlight Pass
- Second Look
- Golden Envelope
- Memory Vault
- one tasteful cosmetic prestige item

That gives you:
- visibility monetization
- second-chance monetization
- reveal-moment monetization
- emotional-memory monetization
- status monetization

Solid spread. Very little clown risk.
